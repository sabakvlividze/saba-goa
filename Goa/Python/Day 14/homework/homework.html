Football

what is football ?

Football, often referred to as soccer, is a globally beloved team sport played between two teams of eleven players each. Players use their feet to kick a ball into the opposing team's goal to score points(known as goals). It's characterized by skillful dribbling, passing, and strategic gameplay, captivating fans worldwide with its passion and drama.

where what football created ?

Football is played on fields or pitches in countries worldwide, from professional stadiums to local parks and schoolyards. It's a universal sport enjoyed by people of all ages and backgrounds, from bustling urban centers to rural villages, fostering a sense of community and passion wherever it's played.
